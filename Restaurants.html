Neighborhood Restaurants


MFK

432 W Diversey Pkwy, Chicago, IL 60614

MFK is a highly rated, Bib Gourmand winning restaurant steps away from Lincoln Park. Their rotating menu features Spanish and Portuguese inspired sea food and unique cocktails in a small but bright setting. It's a great spot to visit any time of year, but particularly in the winter when you need some summer.

The staff are some of the friendliest around - they accept table reservations but we recommend sitting at the bar to get to know the crew. Don't be afraid to say hello to the chefs in the back!


Chilam Balam

3023 N Broadway St, Chicago, IL 60657

Chilam Balam is a sustainably minded Mexican restaurant on a lively stretch of Broadway. They serve shared plates made from fresh ingredients, giving you the opportunity to try a little bit of everything on the menu.

It's a BYOB restaurant, so if you're looking for good wine to bring to dinner, Pastoral Artisan Cheese, Bread and Wine is a great spot to find interesting drink options.


Angelina

3561 N Broadway St, Chicago, IL 60657

Chicago has a lot of neighborhoods Italian restaurants, but Angelina stands out as a dependable option with a warm atmosphere. Their rotating specials are always a good option.

Located on Broadway and Addison only a few blocks to the east of Wrigley field and a few blocks to the west of the lake, it's a great option after watching a Cubs game or taking a walk on the lakefront path.
